Problem Statement

Students at the University of South Carolina struggle to keep track of their retail meal swipes because the balance is only available through a separate app.

Affinity Diagram

This diagram organizes key ideas into clusters to explore different aspects of integrating retail meal swipe tracking into the Grubhub app, including technology, funding, challenges, marketing, and future growth.

Sketches

The three sketches show how retail meal swipes would appear within the Grubhub app or could be better displayed on the current GET app. The first sketch adds a visible swipe balance to the home screen, the second shows the swipe count during checkout, and the third displays a numeric swipe countdown on the GET mobile app Together, they demonstrate a simple and clear way to integrate swipe tracking into the existing interface.
